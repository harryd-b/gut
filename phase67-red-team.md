*Working session, 2026-07-18. ## Referee W (worldsheet/CFT theorist)

*"The carrier identification is cute and the anomaly-arithmetic observation is correct — you've noticed that your flatness condition is Belavin–Knizhnik, which is right and forty years old, and you say so, which I appreciate. Now the problem you've missed: **your D-test is not runnable as posed, because nothing in your own record fixes b.** You say 'b determined by the framework's quantized level' — but I read your phase 48: your candidate for that level (ℓ = GV/4π²) was *refuted by your own test*. Since then, 'the level' floats. Without b, 'compare the census to the degenerate content at b' is a scan, and a scan over b is exactly the AMK manifold-shopping you spent phase 42 condemning. Fix this before anyone runs anything: either derive b from a surviving structure, or restructure D-test as two stages — stage one, show constraints (1)–(4) *jointly determine* b; stage two, the census check at that b. Second: what you call a candidate is, bluntly, the minimal string with extra hopes — Liouville plus c ≤ 1 matter plus ghosts is the most studied non-SM system in existence; your claim that 4D physics lives in the symmetric-orbifold tower over it is carried entirely by your 'join,' which is a conjecture. Say 'minimal string' in the packet or a referee will say it for you, less kindly. Third: where is your **4D Lorentzian metric**? Wedges and modular flows give you local boosts; nobody in sixty-six phases wrote down how ℝ×Σ acquires the actual metric of the world with its actual light cones. That's not a detail — that's a missing chapter. Fourth, smaller: the Virasoro TQFT you lean on lives at c ≥ 25 continuum; your b is supposed to be discrete. The match of those regimes is asserted, not shown. I'd referee a paper on the carrier assembly plus the anomaly observation. I would reject the dictionary paper today."*

Every mention of dark energy in the same manuscript halves your credibility with exactly the people you need."*

## Referee F (Floer/gauge theorist)

*"You asked me to be honest about the statistics table, so: **it has no independently checkable content.** The table is the cohomology of symmetric products — Macdonald's formula — and its identification with the Floer content of Σ×S¹ is Muñoz–Wang, proven. There is nothing left for me to compute that could 'disagree with a row.' The physics content is entirely in your clause identifying homological parity with fermion number — and that's a postulate of yours, not a theorem of mine to check. Your ledger calls this 'desk-decidable, falsifiable by computation'; it is neither — please correct it, because a referee who catches an overstated falsifiability claim stops believing the ledger's honest entries too. What IS a real question: your ESCH balance. Degenerations of Sym^n under nodal limits, tracked against the vanishing-cycle spectrum — that's tractable, moderately interesting, and I can imagine assigning it to a student. The MF↔DHR label join: feasible at the level you claim (labels), already half-done by your own account; the internal dictionary is the hard part and always was."*

## Referee B (CPT-cosmology)

*"Two responses. The modular grounding: genuinely interesting, and I believe your citation-graph check — but as stated it proves too little and claims too much. The thermofield-double/commutant structure is *generic* to any CPT-paired system; what would make it a grounding of Boyle–Turok specifically is a bridge between your two spatial folds and their two temporal sheets across a bang — and your framework has no bang. Until you say precisely what is identified (versus analogized), this is an elegant observation about Tomita theory wearing a cosmology costume. Publishable as the observation; not yet as the grounding. Second, the one that will sting: **P8(a)'s 'universal sign' does not discriminate against the axion explanation, because a single axion field also produces one uniform sign across the sky.** Your magnitude-free selling point is shared with the leading rival. What discriminates is only P8(c) — the EM/GW handedness correlation — which is decades from testable at the required precision. Fix the ledger wording before an axion person fixes it for you in a seminar."*

## The damage report [ranked] and the fixes [applied]

1. **CRITICAL — the b-gap (Referee W).** Internal inconsistency: phase 56's D-test presumes a "quantized level" whose only concrete candidate phase 48 refuted. Unfixed, D-test invites a b-scan — the exact sin the record convicts in others. **Fix (applied to the ledger): D-test restructured to two stages — D-test-0: show constraints (1)–(4) jointly determine b (or prove they cannot); D-test-1: the census comparison at the determined b. The packet's Track W ask is amended accordingly.** The honest status line: the dictionary problem is one constraint-count question *earlier* than the record believed.
2. **SERIOUS — statistics-table falsifiability overstated (Referee F).** D1/P6's "falsifiable by independent Floer computation" is vacuous; the table is proven mathematics and the physics rides on the parity clause [postulate]. **Fix (applied): ledger entry D1 re-worded — the table is *internal consistency made exact*, and its falsifiable face is the clause, testable only through whatever the dictionary makes of fermion number.** The kill column loses a row; better now than in a seminar.
3. **SERIOUS — P8(a) not discriminating (Referee B).** Sign-universality is shared with the axion rival. **Fix (applied): P8(a) re-worded from "discriminator" to "consistency"; discrimination assigned solely to P8(c), with its long horizon stated.**
4. **REQUIRED REFRAMING — the CPT export (Referee B).** State precisely: what is claimed is the operator-algebraic form of *any* CPT-paired cosmology (new); the identification with Boyle–Turok's temporal sheets is open (the bang-bridge). Packet Track B amended.
5. **REQUIRED REFRAMING — GV-T as question, not territory (Referee C);** and the fiber lemma effectively cleared.
6. **MISSING CHAPTER — the 4D Lorentzian reconstruction (Referee W).** The record never explicitly constructs the emergent 3+1 metric with its light cones from (Σ, F, modular data); M1's wedges are local statements. **Logged as the reconstruction chapter — R-chapter — joining the forward program at high priority.** This is the red-team's largest *new* work item.
7. **STRATEGIC — track separation (Referee O).** The mathematics tracks travel alone; the cosmology stays home in first contact. Packet v2 to be split accordingly when sent.
8. Cleared or confirmed by the exercise: the carrier assembly (O), the anomaly observation (W), H6b's formulation (O), ESCH's tractability (F), M-aud3 (C), the concession-sheet strategy (W, O — both referees credited it).

*Status line: the safety check worked — one critical inconsistency (the b-gap) that would have detonated in the first real exchange was found at home, two ledger overstatements were corrected by the standard the ledger itself set, the packet learned to travel in pieces, and the program gained a genuinely new obligation (the reconstruction chapter) from the simulated adversary it would eventually have met in the wild. The packet does not go out as v1. It goes out as v2, after D-test-0's constraint-count is understood — which is, fittingly, one more question the room can pose but only the field can settle.*
