## Decisions the template cannot make (deliberately left to you)

1. **Affiliation line.** "Independent researcher" is honest and adequate; an institutional affiliation should only be used if the institution would recognize the work.
2. **The reply address.** It will receive technical mail, some of it blunt. Choose one you will actually monitor for months.
3. **Whether to name the AI system and version explicitly** (recommended: yes — vagueness reads worse than candor) and whether to offer the full session record on request (recommended: offer it; per the editor, don't front-load it).
4. **The strength word in the cover note's first sentence** — "a research program," not "a theory" or "a discovery." The template recommends; you decide; the record's tags back whichever you choose.
