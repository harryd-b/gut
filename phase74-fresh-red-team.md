## 3. The verdict on convergence — the question this exercise answered

Phase 73 declared the *internal* series converged, with the caveat that self-simulation certifies only self-consistency. The fresh team quantified that caveat: **five substantive findings (FR-1 through FR-5), four of which required literature access from outside the record's frame** — Jabuka–Mark's torsion-sector computations, MOY, Bowen 1977, Ojima/Swanson/the 2024–26 CRT literature, the Möbius-covariance hypotheses of the 2π. The internal rounds could re-derive the record's arithmetic forever and never find these, because the record's blind spots were the referees' blind spots. The two exercises measured different things and both were worth running: the internal rounds debugged the record's *reasoning*; the fresh team debugged its *contact with the literature*. Convergence of the first is necessary and was achieved; convergence of the second has now begun, at the cost of the program's prettiest candidate and its tidiest constant.
