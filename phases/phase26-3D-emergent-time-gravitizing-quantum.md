## 1. The 3D reduction (emergent time)

The emergent-time conclusion (§5.2–§5.3 of the core document) is that the four dimensions are all geometric and **time is not a dimension** — it is the modular (Tomita–Takesaki / thermal-time) flow generated by the Godbillon–Vey stiffness. The natural reformulation is therefore **fundamentally 3-dimensional**:

- **Fundamental object:** a smooth 3-manifold $\Sigma$ (space), carrying its quantum data — the AQFT net, the DHR/superselection sectors, the monopole-Floer homology $HM(\Sigma)$, and a codim-1 foliation (the GV stiffness).
- **Time:** the **emergent modular flow** on the type III$_1$ algebra of $\Sigma$, made dynamical ("gravitized," §2), *not* a fourth coordinate.
- **The 4-manifold is reconstructed:** $\mathbb R\times\Sigma$ with $\mathbb R$ = modular time (and the exotic $\mathbb R^4$ is itself built from a codim-1 foliation of the 3-manifold $S^3$).

This is *faithful*, not a retreat: the entire apparatus was already 3-dimensional — $HM(\Sigma)$, the charge sectors, and the GV foliation all live on $\Sigma$; the "4D" was always $\mathbb R\times\Sigma$. Demoting the $\mathbb R$ (time) to emergent makes the framework cleanly **3-space + emergent time**.

## 3. The experimental grounding (the payoff)

Gravitizing the quantum makes concrete, *currently-tested*, **distinctive-versus-standard-QM** predictions — which our framework inherits via §2:

1. **Higher-order (triple-path) interference, $I_3\neq0$.** Standard QM (Born rule) forces Sorkin's $I_3=0$ *exactly*; a dynamical/nonlinear quantum geometry relaxes the Born rule, giving $I_3\neq0$. Current bounds on $\kappa=I_3/(\sum|I_2|)$: $\kappa=0.006\pm0.012$ (photon multi-slit, Sinha et al. 2010), $0.007\pm0.003$ (NMR, Park et al.); $|\kappa|\lesssim0.01$–$0.02$. Upcoming: **JUNO** neutrino triple-interference, gravitational/atom interferometry.
2. **Optical-lattice atomic-clock redshift corrections, $\alpha\neq0$** in $\Delta\nu/\nu=(1+\alpha)\Delta U/c^2$ (GR gives $\alpha=0$), bounded to $\sim10^{-5}$ and improving.

**Why this matters for the thesis' grounding.** Every prior experimental handle we found was either fitted (masses, inflation), aspirational (Koide), not-forced (generations), or **degenerate with ΛCDM and currently strained** (rigid dark energy $w=-1$ vs DESI). By contrast $I_3\neq0$ and $\alpha\neq0$ are:
- **distinctive** — sharp deviations from *ordinary quantum mechanics*, not from ΛCDM;
- **currently measured** — in the lab, now, with improving precision;
- **on-framework** — they follow from our topology-change / modular structure, not from an unforced assignment.

This is the strongest experimental grounding the program has reached.

## 4. Honest caveats

- **The bridge is conceptual, not derived.** Inheriting $I_3\neq0$ as *our* prediction requires showing our GV-stiffness / topology-change dynamics produces the specific nonlinear evolution — the paper itself only claims $I_3\neq0$ "to some degree."
- **The handle is shared with the whole nonlinear-QG / gravitize-the-quantum class** (Sorkin's test is model-independent). It is distinctive vs standard QM but **not unique to our framework**: a detection would support the whole class; a null would constrain it. Better than degenerate-with-ΛCDM, but not a fingerprint.
- **It requires that our topology change induces nonlinearity.** Standard modular flow is linear/unitary; the nonlinearity needs genuine topology change (exotic transitions). Plausible and on-framework, unproven.
- **The paper is a short essay** (programmatic); the machinery is in the metastring / Born-geometry references (Freidel–Leigh–Minic; Minic–Tze).
