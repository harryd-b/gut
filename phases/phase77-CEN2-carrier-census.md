## 1. The theorem, verbatim [established (others'); OBTAIN-5.6 closed]

> **Theorem 5.6.** *Let Y(g,n) denote the circle bundle with Euler number n > 0 over a surface of genus g. Then, for any choice of [i] ∈ ℤ/nℤ, let j be an integer with minimal absolute value among all integers congruent to i (mod n). We have that* HF⁺_red,∗(Y(g,n), i; ℤ/2ℤ) ≅ ⊕_{s ≡ i (mod n), s ≠ j} X_{c(i,s)}(g, g−1−|s|), *where c(i,s) = d(n,i) − 1 − s + Σ_{0≤t≤s, t≡i (n)} 2t for s ≥ 0 (mirror formula for s ≤ 0), and d(n,i) is the lens-space d-invariant of eq. (2).*

## 2. The carrier census [computed from Thm 5.6; double-anchored]

> **HF⁺_red(T¹Σ_g; ℤ/2) ≅ ℤ/2 — total rank ONE — concentrated in the torsion class [g−1] (the spin-type sector), at grading (4g−9)/4, for every g ≥ 2.** [established (others') + elementary arithmetic; verified g ≤ 6 by direct computation, proved for all g by the window argument]
