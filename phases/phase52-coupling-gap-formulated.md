## 1. The dichotomy, resolved by the record

Two pictures of how the Planckian carrier relates to macroscopic space:

- **(a) Single-fold:** space *is* the carrier at cosmic size. **Excluded by the record:** phase 49 pins the fold scale at ℓ_tr ≈ 0.2–0.3 ℓ_P via the species sum; a cosmic-scale fold would give the wrong G by ~120 orders. (This is phase 44 §2.3's IR constraint, now bidirectional.)
- **(b) Cell picture:** macroscopic space is an aggregate of Planckian fold cells; macroscopic fields are collective modes; **the cosmological modulus is the homogeneous mode of the per-cell Teichmüller parameter.** Selected by elimination. Note the L1 consistency: the fold is locally invisible, so the *only* fold degrees of freedom with macroscopic dynamics are global/collective modes — the homogeneous modulus is exactly the mode L1 permits to matter. [derived-structural]

## 2. The obstacle, quantified: a 10⁻⁶⁰ lightness problem

In the cell picture, the phase 51 potential is per-cell and fold-scale: V'' ~ M_P⁴ in Planck-normalized field space, giving the homogeneous modulus a mass m ~ M_P. Quintessence requires m ~ H₀ ≈ 10⁻³³ eV. **The coupling gap is precisely the requirement that the collective Teichmüller mode be ~10⁻⁶⁰ lighter than its naive mass** — the standard moduli/cosmological-constant problem, arrived at honestly rather than hidden. Any account of the framework that does not state this number is overselling. Two generic escapes fail on arrival: O(1)–O(10⁻²) species cancellations (phase 49's +1) are 58 orders short; and anthropic/landscape moves are off-program (the framework's entire identity is "no dial").

## 3. The program-native mechanism: protection = hierarchy [the new content]

The framework owns one structure with the right *shape* for a 10⁻⁶⁰: exact cancellation plus small breaking. It has been in the record since CORE v1, doing a different job:

**Paired spectra decouple from the potential.** In a supersymmetric-paired sector, bosonic and fermionic determinants cancel in the free energy; the paired part of the spectrum contributes a *moduli-independent* — exactly flat — piece to V(τ). Hence, structurally:

> **The Zograf–Takhtajan potential of phase 51 is generated entirely by the *unprotected* spectrum. The protected stratum contributes nothing to the force on the modulus.** [derived-structural at physics level; the precise pairing statement for the fold's spectrum is audit item **L1-aud**]

**The consequence, if it holds at one loop and beyond:** the potential's scale is not M_P⁴ but (gap of the lightest unprotected physics)⁴-adjacent — a *structured* hierarchy in which the modulus is light because almost everything that could weigh it down is protected, and the protection is Plank IV's own.
