## 2. The second-handle inventory [each verified where it touches literature]

Registered as R-check-1**: two independent determinations of one number, the framework's version of the GUT-scale triangulation, contingent (as the verification insists) on the assumed content along the way.

**(b) The primordial window — real, narrow, far [conditional and honest].** Trans-Planckian physics can imprint log-oscillations on the primordial spectrum (Martin–Brandenberger; Danielsson — verified, one arXiv-prefix corrected). The honest arithmetic, all verified: in the *optimistic* (non-Bunch-Davies boundary) scenario the amplitude is linear, H_inf/Λ ≲ **10⁻⁶** given r < 0.032 and Λ = ħc/R ≈ 5 m_P — and note the framework's sub-Planckian R makes the signal *five times smaller* than a Planck-scale cutoff would. Sensitivities: Planck ~10⁻², CMB-S4 ~10⁻³, idealized dark-ages 21 cm tomography 10⁻⁶–10⁻⁷. **Registered as R-check-2:** a named window at the edge of an unfunded instrument, live only if the linear scenario holds and only if the framework commits to a boundary condition at the fold — which the dictionary would have to supply. Generations away; registered anyway, because registering is free and fitting is not.
