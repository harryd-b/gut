## 1. The verified imports [all verified against primary sources this session]

- **(H1) Katok entropy rigidity (surfaces).** For a smooth negatively curved metric on a closed surface S of fixed area, h_Liouville ≤ (2π|χ|/V)^{1/2} ≤ h_top with *both* inequalities strict unless the curvature is constant; in particular **h_Liouville = h_top ⟺ constant curvature**. [Katok, ETDS 2 (1982) 339. Two-dimensional theorem; the higher-dimensional analogue is the open *Katok conjecture*.]
- **(H2) Unique measure of maximal entropy.** Transitive Anosov flows have a unique MME — the Bowen–Margulis measure. [Margulis 1970; Bowen, Amer. J. Math. 94 (1972); Math. Systems Theory 7 (1974).]
- **(H3) Liouville as equilibrium state.** For the (volume-preserving) Anosov geodesic flow, the Liouville measure is the SRB measure and is the **unique equilibrium state of the geometric potential** φ^u (unstable-Jacobian rate), with pressure zero. [Bowen–Ruelle, Invent. Math. 29 (1975), Thm 3.3 + Prop 4.4; the Liouville-=-SRB step is the standard volume-preserving specialization, stated explicitly.]
- **(H4) Cohomological dictionary.** Two Hölder potentials have the same equilibrium state iff cohomologous up to a constant; hence **Liouville = Bowen–Margulis ⟺ φ^u ~ const** (Livšic: iff every closed geodesic carries the same normalized unstable exponent). The step from there to *constant curvature* is Katok's theorem again — geometric rigidity, not dynamics. [Standard equilibrium-state theory + Livšic; guard: do **not** cite Butler (JDG 109 (2018)) for surfaces — his rigidity theorems are the higher-dimensional analogue.]
- **(H5) KMS ↔ measures.** For groupoid C*-algebras with dynamics generated by a real cocycle c, KMS_β states correspond to quasi-invariant measures with Radon–Nikodym cocycle e^{−βc}; cohomologous cocycles yield the same KMS structure. [Renault 1980; Kumjian–Renault, PAMS 134 (2006); Neshveyev, JOT 70 (2013).] The specifically-Anosov C*-statement ("the Ruelle algebra has its canonical KMS state at β = h_top given by Bowen–Margulis") is PLAUSIBLE with the route via Markov coding → Ruelle algebras [Putnam–Spielberg, JFA 163 (1999)] but **needs a pinned citation — logged as audit item H5b**; the assembly below is phrased so that only the measure-level statements (theorems) carry weight.
